Overview

How might we help Ballot Box design their landing page?

Ballot Box is a platform that brings politicians and people closer together. The people raise issues that they care about. The politicians submit their stances on these issues. The people then give feedback to the politicians on their policies and stances. Ballot Box is an up and coming mobile start-up that needed a website to promote their mobile app which is launching soon.

My goal was to help Ballot Box create a landing page that felt modern and clean while catering towards its target audiences to spread awareness of the app.

Client
Ballot Box

Duration

Team / Role

Initial focus

From App to Website

I was challenged in thinking of ways to design and create a website that maintained the look and feel of Ballot Box’s app. I chose to use the same branding and style guide in terms of colour and font throughout the website to keep a sense of unity across all platforms.

With the app currently in development, I opted for a single page site to minimise navigation clutter and instead, allow people to engage with the content with minimal distractions.

Focus on what's important

Visual Hierarchy

I decided to use white space to allow for readers to have more breathing room to easily digest the contents of the page, calling out key concepts of the app. Cards were also implemented to help with visual hierarchy, also allowing the site to scale into a mobile-friendly view seamlessly.

Blocking out each part

Clear separations between sections

I implemented a colour blocking design on the landing page to clearly separate each section and call out information related to different types of readers and users. For example, having different coloured sections which were “for the people” and “for the politicians” to differentiate how they are able to use the app.

Keep scalability in mind

Always design to accomodate mobile

When creating the footer, the thought of being scalable was a top priority. By dividing the footer into 2, it once again allowed for a clear separation of different sections as well as making it a lot easier to scale it down for mobile without any issues.

After the design was finalised, I transported the design into Wix to allow for an easy handover to Ballot Box. This allowed them to easily edit and add content to the site without the need for a technical coded solution.

Takeaways

Key Learnings

Cater to the needs of the client

There are multiple ways to design a landing page, and the design I implemented catered to what Ballot Box wanted to share as their platform’s purpose to each of their user segments. Ballot Box wanted to focus on the types of users of its app and the features available to each of them, hence I focused on a clear and visual design that would celebrate this.
