About me

Hi, I’m Ron, a Developer turned UI / UX Designer. Growing up, I always had a love for technology, problem solving and helping people. My career as a Developer helped satiate my desire for problem solving allowing me to gain a greater appreciation of best development practices. But, I realised my love of problem solving was enhanced when I got to solve problems in a group setting. I always found myself leaning to the frontend side of development, enjoying coding pixel perfect designs and interactions on a page.

But my moment of clarity came when I first worked with a UX Designer on a project, and for the first time I was able to understand the reasons for building what I was building. In essence, I was able to finally understand the ‘why’ behind the ‘what’ that I had been doing. I loved how a UX designer was able to facilitate the communications between business, technology and design to help design with both the users and business goals in mind. This sparked my interest in UX, and paved the first steps to my new career.

I’m now looking to further evolve myself as a UI / UX designer, tying in my previous dev knowledge together with my UX knowledge to help facilitate the communications and processes to create a seamless journey for all parties across future projects.
