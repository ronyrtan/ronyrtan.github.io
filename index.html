This is me.

Growing up, I always had a love for technology and problem solving. I was the type of kid that would take apart pieces of technology just to reconstruct it so I could understand it better. This fundamental love of problem solving lead me to start building computers for fun and thus took off my passion for technology.

After graduating from the University of Technology Sydney (UTS) at the end of 2012, with a Bachelors of Science in Information Technology, I found myself landing a job in a field totaly unrelated to my degree. For the next few years I worked at the Star Casino as a croupier, not living up to my potential or doing what I was passionate about, but it paid the bills.

After months on end of seriously considering the direction of my life, I decided I wanted to do something that I could wake up to with a smile and be proud of. Something that kept coming to mind was my love of technology and the area of study I thoroughly enjoyed and was good at in university, programming and development. With my passion rekindled, I made up my mind and decided to quit my job to pursue a career change in Web Development.

After carefully considering my options of study, my best friend recommended me to enroll in Generally Assembly's Web Development Immersive course and I never regretted a second of it. Throughout the course I was able to overcome many challenges and create websites and apps that I never thought I would be able to. After overcoming each challenge I felt that this was definitely the path I wanted to take, and sought even greater challenges to further myself as a developer. I look forward to working with an employer who can help me constantly learn and grow, allowing me to use my skills to help develop their projects.
