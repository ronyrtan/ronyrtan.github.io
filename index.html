This is me.

After graduating from the University of Technology Sydney (UTS) at the end of 2012, with a bachelors in Information Technology, I found myself landing a job in a field totaly unrelated to my degree. For the next few years I worked at the Star Casino as a croupier, not living up to my potential or doing what I was passionate about, but it paid the bills.

After months on end of seriously considering the direction of my life, I decided I wanted to do something that I could wake up to with a smile and be proud of. Something that kept coming to mind was the one thing I thoroughly enjoyed doing and was good at in university, programming. With that, I made up my mind and decided to quit my job and pursue a career change in Web Development.

After carefully considering my options of study, I enrolled in Generally Assembly's Web Development Immersive course and never regretted a second of it. Throughout the course I was able to overcome many challenges and create websites and apps that I never thought I would be able to. After overcoming each challenge I felt that this was definitely the path I wanted to take, and sought even greater challenges to further myself as a developer. I am looking forward to working with an employer who can help me constantly learn and grow, allowing me to use my skills to help develop their projects.
