I’m a Developer turned UI / UX Designer.

I'm a Mid weight Product Designer with 3 years of digital agency and freelancing experience, having worked across all stages of a project, from requirements gathering and blueprinting, to delivering high-fidelity screens. I enjoy utilising my empathetic attitude and adaptability to creating meaningful, human-centred designs!
