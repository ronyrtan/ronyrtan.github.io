Overview

How might we help Wrk+ modernise their current dashboard?

Paper and Wire was engaged by Wrk+ to help update their current dashboard's look and feel to be in line with their new branding guidelines. Our goal was to help Wrk+ make their dashboard site look and feel modern as well as bring consistency throughout the site.

Client
Wrk+

Duration
4 weeks
Team

- UX Designer

Role
UX Designer
Methods
Analysis
Sketching
Wireframing & Prototyping

Where is our main focus?

Data visualisation

We were challenged in thinking of ways to modernise and rebrand their current working software, focusing on visual clarity and how to better call out important information.

A card base was implemented within the new design to help bring up to date the look and feel of the dashboard as well to enable information and callouts to be scaled where needed.

Insights and Surveys

Reducing clutter to form clarity

With so much information being held within the insights reports and survey questions, we strived to create a minimal clean style where people’s eyes wouldn’t be distracted and be lost in unnecessary visual clutter.

Access Panel

Consistency keeps people engaged

Something that was highly prioritised in the update was consistency. In the old versions, the access panel and the dashboard not only had completely different structures but a totally different color scheme which didn’t match the brand. By bringing both sections under one uniform style a more complete feel was achieved.

Takeaways

Key Learnings

The importance of clear data visualisation

There are various ways in which one can display data and information. Giving multiple queues can help reinforce and call out important information so that people can easily access the information needed for their jobs. Don't make people have to look for the important information they need.
